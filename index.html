The founder
NRJI is the culmination of two decades proving extraordinary performance is not reserved for the elite. It can be trained.
Sophie Storm Roberts Founder, NRJI

This all started with one question

What if we train our energy the way athletes train their bodies?

From no athletic background, Sophie Storm Roberts became a world-first endurance athlete by learning to stay present, courageous and curious under extreme pressure.

She became the only person to climb the highest mountains in all eight Alpine countries, and cycle between them, in 32 days. She is a global keynote speaker at TEDx and Harvard.

Founder of L2P24, helping 1,000+ people cycle London to Paris in 24 hours, and TrailBlazers, building courage in teenage girls. Coach to founders, leaders and athletes.
